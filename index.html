Yeast Systems Group (YSG)

What is YSG?

We are a small group of scientists from the Boston area (and primarily Broad Institute) who meet to informally discuss topics related to systems biology, using yeast as a model organism. We're not a lecture series or journal club. We start each meeting with a roundtable discussion, where anyone in the group can bring up experimental questions, problems, or discuss new data. The goal is for YSG to function as if we're all members of the same lab, who meet regularly and informally enough to be helpful. We then typically have a longer talk by one speaker. The talks can be chalk talks or standard slide talks.
